A bit about Apex Legends itself

In the wake of hype raised by PUBG and Fortnite, everyone strives to break into a new Battle Royale genre - from major publishers to unknown studios. One of those who decided to try to create a worthy competitor to these games was EA with Respawn Entertainment and their game - Apex Legends. The game is available on PC, PlayStation Xbox, iOS, Android, Nintendo Switch. There are two basic rules in any royal battle: a narrowing and damaging circle does not allow you to sit in one place and inexorably push players against each other and at the end of the round there should be only one player (or one team). Apex Legends does not encroach on these unshakable foundations of the genre, but rather boldly experimenting with others. The most noticeable difference from other games is the presence of various heroes or as they are called Legends. Each Legend has a unique appearance, skills and abilities. Developers managed to create truly unique characters - both externally and in terms of gameplay. They resemble the Overwatch characters, except for restrictions on the use of weapons and equipment - everyone is equal here.
